Terms & Conditions

Before committing to a course with us, please be sure to read through our short terms and conditions carefully.

Group Lessons

  • Mixed classes cannot be refunded nor can fees be rolled over for other group or private lessons.
  • Refunds are not provided for missed classes, however, if you are unable to make a class, contact us and we will try to find an alternative one (if available) for that week.
  • Classes are kept to a maximum of eight (8) students. Occasionally, your class may have an extra student or two if they are doing a catch-up lesson (see above), however the class size will never exceed 10 students.
  • A minimum of five students is required to run a class. We will contact you (should your class not go ahead) to try and arrange an alternative
  • In the event your class does not run due to lack of participants, you will receive a full refund

Group Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellation more than 14 calendar days prior to the first day of term: Full refund of any monies paid
  • Cancellation less than 14 calendar days prior to the first day of term: Full credit†
  • Cancellation on or after first day of term: No refunds, sorry.
    • †Valid for six months, non transferable.

Private Lessons

  • Private lessons can be paid for individually or in bundles of 10.
  • Paying for 10 lessons upfront attracts a discount of 10%.
  • 10 lessons must be used within a 13-week period.
